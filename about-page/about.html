• BROK'S CHIKAAN

AN ECOSYSTEM OF DELICIOUSNESS HAS BEEN TALKED ABOUT RECENTLY!
Founded in March 2024, Brok’s Chikaan is a place where delicious meals and exciting conversation meet. Brok's Chikaan, co-founded by two passionate persons, comes up to its name, which is inspired from the Filipino word "chika" which means “to talk about something” and “chikaan” which means a gathering spot for casual conversation and storytelling.
